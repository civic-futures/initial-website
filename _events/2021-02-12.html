--- title: Predicting High School Dropout during a Pandemic: A Simulation Study on the Impact of Concept Drift date: February 11, 2021 time: 9:00am - 10:00am location-1: Virtual Event location-2: location-url: register-link: https://ldwews.eventbrite.com/ register-text: Register on Eventbrite --- The COVID-19 pandemic has caused massive disruptions to risk-prediction models trained on longitudinal data. Join us for a talk with Paul Xu, Data Scientist at The Policy Lab during Brown's Love Data Week. Paul will focus on the issue of concept drift and its effect on the model implemented in the Early Warning System (EWS), which the Rhode Island Department of Education (RIDE) uses to predict public high school students' risks of dropping out.
